Add a new deck

Go to Google Sheets and create a new "Blank" spreadsheet.

Type or paste in the prompts you want to memorize, one card per row in the spreadsheet. The first column is the prompt, and the second column is the text that you are trying to memorize.

You must have one header row at the top of your sheet.

You may have multiple sheets in your document to study them individually.

Click the "File" menu at the top left, and choose "Publish to web...".

Click "Publish".

Don't try to use the link that they give you here, it won't work. It is very important that you share your spreadsheet first.

Close the "Publish to the Web" dialog. Click the "Share" button at the top right.

Click "Get shareable link".
